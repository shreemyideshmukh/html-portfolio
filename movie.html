MY TOP FAV MOVIES

swapped

Swapped is an animated fantasy movie about two friends who experience a magical body swap after a mysterious event. They must work together, understand each other’s lives, and find a way to reverse the swap. The movie focuses on friendship, adventure, and self-discovery.

The Princess and the Frog (2009)

A young woman named Tiana is transformed into a frog after a magical encounter with a prince. She goes on an adventure through a magical world while discovering love, courage, and dreams

Strange Magic (2015)

A fantasy animated movie about fairies, goblins, and magical creatures whose lives change because of a powerful love potion. It has magic, adventure, and a colorful fantasy world
